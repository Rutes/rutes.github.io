Game Servers

Rutes - Minecraft avatar

I host some game servers (Valheim, Enshrouded, Minecraft, etc).
They are free - I do not charge any kind of fee, I do not accept donations.
All you have to do is ask nicely and agree to follow the rules.
To request access to the servers, join Discord and follow the instructions.

If you are unable to join Discord, then you or your parents may instead send me an email.

Please read and abide by the rules if you want to join my servers:

1. Be respectful

Do not be rude to other players or staff members
Do not swear or curse excessively
No excessive CAPS LOCK in any channels
- Also please avoid extended single-character exclamations
(i.e., do not "REEEEEE", "oooof", "loooool", etc.)
No begging, no asking for special permissions, etc.
- do not ask for free items
- do not repeatedly ask for "trades" or "does anyone have X?"
Do not whine and complain.
Do not discuss sensitive topics in server chat channels.
Keep religion, politics, triggering topics, etc. OUT of the server chat channels

2. Be ethical.

Do not steal from other players.
Do not bully other players.
- only PvP with consent
- please do not spawn camp, grief, be excessive, etc.
- if a player is in their own base, do not attack them, unless it is a consensual staged raid thing

3. Use common sense.

Do not cheat.
- exploiting bugs, glitches, etc are not allowed
- let a member of staff know if you discover an exploit
- cheating tools/methods such as X-Ray are NOT allowed
- if you are caught cheating you will be BANNED

Join the fun

If you agree to abide by the rules, then you may join the Discord to apply for access.

I also used to manage some modpacks for Minecraft, but they have been dormant for many years. They can still be viewed on CurseForge.
